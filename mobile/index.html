Instrument Maker Mobile

This is a demo page for Instrument Maker Mobile applications. Please follow these instructions from your iPad or iPhone:
  • Download MobMuPlat from the app store.
  • Download the examples from this website.
    (If it doesn't open straight away, you might want to try again).
  • When prompted, press "download to MobMuPlat". If you are given another option (e.g. ThumbJam), press "more" to find MobMuPlat.
  • Tap the menu in the top-left-hand corner of the screen to load an instrument.
  • Choose one of the instruments with an emoji, e.g. the monkey, to open an Instrument Maker example.
